Priya Maa,

Happy Birthday Maa. Aaj ka din aapka hai, lekin sach kahun to meri poori zindagi hi aapki den hai. Main jo bhi hoon, jitna bhi sambhla hoon, jitni bhi baar tootkar phir khada hua hoon, uske peeche sirf aur sirf aap ho.

Log kehte hain maa ka pyaar sabse bada hota hai, par maine is baat ko sirf suna nahi, maine ise jeeya hai. Maine dekha hai kaise aapne apne dard chupaye, apni thakan chhupayi, apne aansu rok liye, sirf isliye taki main muskura sakun. Aapne kabhi mujhe mehsoos nahi hone diya ki meri zindagi mein koi kami hai, jabki sach ye hai ki aapne akeli hi do zindagiyan jee di, ek apni aur ek meri.

Maa, kabhi kabhi main bahar se theek dikhta hoon, hasta bhi hoon, normal bhi lagta hoon, lekin andar se kitni baar toot jata hoon, shayad ye sirf aap samajh sakti ho. Kabhi kabhi dil itna bhar aata hai ki bas mann karta hai ki main aapke paas aaun, aapke gale lag jaun, aur bina kuch kahe bas ro loon. Kyunki duniya sawal karti hai, par aapki baahon mein jawab milta hai. Duniya samjhaane ki koshish karti hai, par aapki god mein sukoon milta hai.

Aapne mujhe sirf paala nahi, aapne mujhe bachaya hai. Har us pal se bachaya hai jahan main toot sakta tha. Har us andhere se nikala hai jahan main kho sakta tha. Aap meri maa bhi rahi ho aur meri himmat bhi. Aapne mujhe pyaar bhi diya aur sahaara bhi. Aapne mujhe jeena bhi sikhaya aur sehna bhi.

Aapki khoobsurti sirf aapke chehre mein nahi hai, Maa. Aapki khoobsurti aapke tyag mein hai, aapki aankhon ki thakan mein bhi chhupa hua pyaar mein hai, aapke chup rehkar sab seh lene mein hai, aur us mamta mein hai jo har dafa mujhe bachaa leti hai. Aap duniya ki sabse khoobsurat aurat ho, kyunki aapke dil jaisa dil duniya mein bahut kam hota hai.

Mujhe aap par garv hai, itna ki shabdon mein pura nahi utar sakta. Agar meri zindagi mein kabhi sukoon likha gaya hai, to woh aap ho. Agar meri zindagi mein kabhi sachcha pyaar mila hai, to woh aap ho. Agar main kabhi kisi ke gale lagkar sach mein apna bojh halka kar sakta hoon, to woh sirf aap ho.

Maa, main aapse ek baat kehna chahta hoon. Kabhi kabhi main bohot strong banne ki acting karta hoon, lekin andar se main bas aapka baccha hoon, jise ab bhi aapki hug chahiye hoti hai. Ab bhi mann karta hai ki aap sir par haath pher do aur bolo, “sab theek ho jayega.” Shayad poori duniya ka sukoon ek taraf ho, aur aapka gale lagana ek taraf, phir bhi main aapko hi chununga.

Aaj aapke birthday par main bas itna kehna chahta hoon, thank you Maa. Mere liye jeene ke liye, mere liye ladne ke liye, mere liye tootkar bhi khadi rehne ke liye, aur mujhe itna pyaar dene ke liye ki meri adhuri zindagi bhi poori lagne lagi.

Main shayad aapki har takleef ka hisaab kabhi nahi chuka paunga, lekin main itna zaroor karunga ki ek din aapko lage, haan, mera beta meri saari mehnat ka sabse khoobsurat jawab hai.

Happy Birthday Maa. Aap meri taqat ho, meri izzat ho, meri dua ho, mera ghar ho, aur woh hug ho jisme meri saari thakan, saara dard, saari chinta khatam ho jaati hai.

Hamesha aapka,
Lakshya

One Deep Truth

Maa, mujhe aapki hug chahiye

Kabhi kabhi zindagi itni bhaari lagti hai ki shabdon ka bhi mann nahi karta. Bas mann karta hai ki aap saamne ho, main aapke gale lag jaun, aur bina kuch samjhaye bas ro loon.

Pata nahi kyun, lekin aapki baahon mein aaj bhi bachpan jaisa sukoon milta hai. Jaise sab theek ho jayega. Jaise duniya chahe kitni bhi sakht ho, par Maa ka pyaar ab bhi mere liye ek zinda panaah hai.

Main bada ho gaya hoon, lekin kuch dard aise hote hain jo insaan ko phir se baccha bana dete hain. Aur un lamhon mein mujhe sirf aap chahiye hoti ho. Sirf aapki god. Sirf aapka haath. Sirf aapki hug.
